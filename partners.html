Our Partners

A strong network of sponsors, mentors, and community partners around our students.

The Grant That Opened the Door

NASA made the final step possible.

The NASA grant covered our FRC registration and our first regional — which is what turned a planned team into a competing one. Everything our program had built over the past three seasons was ready for FRC. The grant is what let our students actually walk through the door.

The Rookie All Star and Judges' Awards came from what they did once they were inside.
